Fresh Tap Water

Description

This recipe creates a deliciously refreshing, low calorie, beverage with minimal preparation time. Even your kids will be able to make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup
  • 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Place glass under the tap.
  2. Turn tap on.
  3. Turn tap off.
  4. Top up glass with additional teaspoons of water until full.
